Emergency Guide

Tap any card to expand. Designed to work even without internet — bookmark this page.

🌊Flood›
  • Move to higher ground immediately
  • Avoid walking or driving in moving water
  • Turn off electricity at the main switch
  • Keep a waterproof bag with essentials
  • Call 112 or NDRF (1078) for evacuation
🏚️Earthquake›
  • Drop, cover, and hold on
  • Stay away from windows and heavy furniture
  • Do NOT use elevators
  • Check for gas leaks after shaking stops
  • Expect aftershocks — stay prepared
🌡️Heatwave›
  • Stay indoors between 12 PM – 4 PM
  • Drink water every 30 minutes
  • Wear light, loose, light-coloured clothing
  • Check on elderly and young children
  • Avoid strenuous outdoor activity
🌀Cyclone›
  • Stay indoors and away from windows
  • Store water and non-perishable food
  • Secure loose objects outside
  • Follow evacuation orders immediately
  • Do not go out during the eye of the storm
🔥Fire›
  • Evacuate immediately — don't use lifts
  • Crawl low under smoke
  • Close doors to slow fire spread
  • Call Fire Brigade: 101
  • Never re-enter a burning building
⚡Lightning / Storm›
  • Stay indoors or in a hard-topped vehicle
  • Avoid tall, isolated trees
  • Keep away from metal fences and wires
  • Unplug electronics during lightning
  • Wait 30 min after last thunder before going out
